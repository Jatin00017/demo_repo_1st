Physics

Physics is the scientific study of matter, energy, and the fundamental forces that govern the behavior of the universe.
It seeks to understand the fundamental principles and laws that describe the physical world, from the smallest
subatomic particles to the vast expanses of the cosmos. Physics is a fundamental science that underpins many other
scientific disciplines and has led to numerous technological advancements that have shaped our modern world.

Maths

Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, structures, patterns, and relationships. It is a fundamental
and universal language that allows us to describe, analyze, and solve problems in various fields, from
science and engineering to economics and social sciences. Mathematics plays a crucial role in understanding and navigating the world around us

HTML

HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) is the standard markup language used to create web pages. It provides the
structure and content of a web page by using a system of elements and tags. HTML is a key component of web
development and is essential for creating and displaying web content.
